MAGIC BOXES

Welcome to Magic Boxes, a whimsical web project that lets you unlock the magic of customizable boxes!

Start by typing '/box' in your browser. You'll see a single box appear. To make things even more exciting, add a number after '/box,' like '/box/4.' Suddenly, lots of boxes will pop up! For a touch of magic, try adding a color after the number, such as '/box/4/green.' The boxes will change color right before your eyes!
